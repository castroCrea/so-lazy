Senior Full Stack Engineer

Can you remember the first time you felt the superpower of using a CLI?

We want to bring this delightful experience - that engineers enjoy daily - to everyone.

CLIs can be intimidating and feel like a black box for non-technical people and thus were never really adopted widely because of their learning curve.

But at Lazy we strongly believe that we can reunify the experience of a CLI with a classical GUI to build what we call a CLUI: an intuitive interface with a small learning curve that blends GUIs and CLIs to perform actions in the quickest and most intuitive way possible.

Today CLIs are back, but hidden under the name of search and we think it's the perfect time to lend cachet to all the efficiency of the original CLIs.

With that in mind, we launched the first version of Lazy and became the IoT Product of the Year 2019 on Product Hunt.

We're now applying that same UX paradigm to the world of ideas, thoughts and tasks: the next level of Lazy is a smart CLUI that captures a thought with its context at the speed of a keyboard shortcut.

Often when you are in the middle of something, a thought comes to mind that you need to write down, whether it's a lightbulb moment or a reminder. With Lazy, you can write down that thought with a UX that reduces the cognitive cost of jotting something down, respecting your precious flow, and always designed for speed because the most valuable thing in the world is time.
